# Write Programs in Plain English
Sure, you can prompt your way deep into the wood, but how did you get there? What prompts got you to this point? How do you make sense of your systems when you no longer has a grasp on your code? These are the sorts of problems `engl` seeks to solve.

## What If Programs Read Like Novels?
I was listening to Computerphile on YouTube and a phrase stood out to me. "There comes a time when a system can no longer fit within the head of a single person", or something like that.

Programs are forgotten; stories are remembered.

## Everything is a String
In `engl`, strings are first-class citizens. No need to wrap them in quotes at all.
